Fairview

Fairview is a GPU(Graphics Processing Unit) accelerated Machine learning framework that allows to capture packets from network interfaces or obtain network data from a capture file and perform real time analysis,that helps telecom operators to arrest revenue leakage.

IPTV

IP based TV service is delivered using the Internet Protocol (IP) technology. It is the same technology used to deliver Internet on your PC. For IPTV service, you require a broadband connection, a digital Set-Top-Box and a TV.

IPTV offers services such as hundreds of digital quality channels (standard definition (SD)and Hi-Definition (Hi-Def), video on demand, digital video recording, interactive applications etc. Note: IPTV is not internet TV viewed on your PC. Neither it is internet browsing on TV.

2talk

2talk's VoIP service uses your Internet connection to provide your phone service. Amazing rates and a reliable, quality phone connection, makes switching to 2talk an easy choice. Choose from pay as you go or a monthly plan that bundles lines and minutes, also, you can build your own SIP trunk by mixing and matching our calling plans.
